Facts

    Carbon Footprint Data

  • The average US household produces 7.5 tons of CO2 equivalents per year.
  • Transportation (27% of 2020 greenhouse gas emissions) – The transportation sector generates the largest share of greenhouse gas emissions. (More Info)
  • we only have 50 to 250 years to change our actions before climate change is irreversible

    Effect of co2 on the environment

  • Cause climate change by trapping heat,
  • Contributes to respiratory disease from smog and air pollution.
  • Ice is melting worldwide, especially at the Earth’s poles.
  • Global sea levels are rising 0.13 inches (3.2 millimeters) a year.
  • Rising temperatures are affecting wildlife and their habitats.
  • Many species of animals are migrating from their presiding location causing a possible extinction of multiple rare species
  • Hurricanes and other storms are likely to become stronger.
  • Less freshwater will be available
  • Many diseases will spread

    How does reuse reduce carbon footprint?

  • Project Drawdown estimates that recycling between 2020 and 2050 will reduce emissions by 5.5-6.02 gigatons of carbon dioxide (equivalent to taking over 1 billion cars off the streets for one year)
  • Reusing/recycling reduces greenhouse gas by reducing energy consumption less energy is needed to extract, transport and process materials to manufacture products.
  • waste prevention and recycling paper products allows more trees to remain standing
