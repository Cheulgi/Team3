Donating to Detroit Donate Up

In today's world, the average person's carbon footprint is close to 4 tons. And we want to reduce that. Our orginization is focused on helping the world reduce the size of their carbon footprint, by allowing individuals. like yourself, to donate items that are no longer in use to others, right in your area. All you have to do is:

  1. List your item in the provided spaces
  2. Provide your location for said item, so people in your area can see your listing
  3. Set a pickup/meeting time for your seller, and
  4. Receive bonuses and points for your sale!
We only have one Earth, and we want to take care of it for future generations to come! Help us keep Mother Earth healthy and thriving by joining our organization and donating today!
